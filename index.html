Before You Go

Essential Travel Tips

🗓️

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for most regions. Avoid monsoon season (June–September) for hill stations and beaches.

💰

Budget Planning

India is incredibly affordable. Budget ₹1,500–₹3,000/day for backpackers; luxury trips can run ₹15,000+/day.

📋

Visa & Entry

Most nationalities can apply for an e-Visa online. Apply at least 4 days before travel at indianvisaonline.gov.in.

🚆

Getting Around

Indian Railways is vast and affordable. Book on IRCTC. Autos, buses and flights connect cities efficiently.
