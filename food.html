A Culinary Universe

Indian Food Guide

Spices, aromas, and a thousand regional traditions — eating in India is a journey in itself.

Must Try

Iconic Indian Dishes

Your bucket-list of flavours — dishes that define India's extraordinary culinary heritage.

🍛
Pan India

Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with spiced meat or vegetables, slow-cooked in the dum style. Hyderabadi, Lucknowi, Kolkata, Malabar — every city swears by its own version.

🌶️🌶️🌶️
🫓
Punjab

Butter Chicken & Naan

Creamy tomato-butter gravy with tender chicken, mopped up with pillowy tandoor-baked naan. The world's most beloved Indian dish.

🌶️🌶️
🥘
South India

Masala Dosa

Crispy fermented rice crepe filled with spiced potato masala, served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar. A South Indian breakfast institution.

🌶️🌶️
🍢
Delhi / Mumbai

Pani Puri / Golgappa

Crispy hollow spheres filled with spiced tamarind water, chickpeas, and mint chutney. The ultimate street food — an explosion in every bite.

🌶️🌶️🌶️
🍮
Rajasthan

Dal Baati Churma

Hard baked wheat balls (baati) dunked in spiced lentil soup (dal), alongside sweet churma. The soul food of Rajasthan's desert.

🌶️
🍣
Goa

Fish Curry & Rice

Goan fish curry with coconut milk, kokum, and dried red chillies alongside steamed red rice — the simple, perfect soul of coastal Goa.

🌶️🌶️

Around the Country

Regional Cuisines

🏔️

North Indian

Rich gravies, tandoor breads, kebabs, biryanis. Heavy use of dairy — cream, ghee, paneer. Mughal-influenced richness.

Butter Chicken · Rogan Josh · Sarson da Saag · Chole Bhature
🌴

South Indian

Rice-based, coconut-heavy, tamarind-tangy. Fermented foods dominate: idli, dosa. Banana leaf thali is a feast.

Dosa · Idli · Sambar · Rasam · Chettinad Chicken
🏖️

West Indian

From Gujarati sweet-and-sour thalis to Goan spiced seafood to Maharashtrian street food — enormous diversity.

Dhokla · Vada Pav · Pav Bhaji · Thepla · Vindaloo
🌿

East Indian

Subtle spicing, mustard oil, fish and rice. Bengal's sweets are legendary. Northeastern cuisine is unique with bamboo shoots and fermented flavours.

Machher Jhol · Rasgulla · Momos · Litti Chokha

Eat on the Go

Street Food Guide

🥙
Kathi Roll — Kolkata's gift to the world: egg-wrapped flatbread filled with spiced meat.
🧆
Samosa — The ubiquitous crispy triangle stuffed with spiced potato or meat. Best with tamarind chutney.
🌮
Vada Pav — Mumbai's beloved spiced potato fritter in a soft bun. The Indian burger.
🍲
Chole Bhature — Fluffy deep-fried bread with spicy chickpea curry. A Delhi breakfast institution.
🍦
Kulfi — Dense, creamy Indian ice cream in flavours like pistachio, rose, and mango. Served on a stick.
🥤
Lassi — Thick yoghurt drink, sweet or salted. Amritsar's matka lassi in an earthen pot is unmissable.
🫚
Jalebi — Spiral-shaped, sugar-syrup-soaked fried batter. Eaten hot, crispy, with rabri or chai.
☕
Masala Chai — Spiced tea with cardamom, ginger, and cinnamon. The heartbeat of every Indian morning.
