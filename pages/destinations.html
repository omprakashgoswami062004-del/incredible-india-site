Explore the Land

Top Destinations

Discover India's most breathtaking places — from golden deserts to misty mountains.

🕌 North India

Taj Mahal, Agra

Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, the Taj Mahal is the world's most iconic monument of love. Best visited at sunrise for that golden glow.

📅 Oct – Feb ⭐ UNESCO World Heritage 🕐 3–4 hrs
🏯 Rajasthan

Jaipur — Pink City

The capital of Rajasthan dazzles with its pink-hued architecture, Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, and bustling bazaars filled with gems, textiles, and handicrafts.

📅 Oct – Feb ⭐ Royal Heritage 🕐 2–3 days
🌸 Kashmir

Dal Lake, Srinagar

Spend nights on a traditional houseboat on Dal Lake, explore Mughal gardens, and sip kahwa as the mountains glow at dusk. Heaven on earth.

📅 apr – jun ⭐ Paradise Valley 🕐 3–5 days
⛰️ Himachal Pradesh

Manali & Spiti Valley

Adventure seekers flock to Manali for mountain passes, rafting, and skiing. Spiti Valley offers stark lunar landscapes, ancient monasteries, and star-filled skies.

📅 jun – sep ⭐ Adventure Hub 🕐 5–7 days
🧘 Uttarakhand

Rishikesh & Haridwar

The yoga capital of the world sits on the banks of the Ganges. Watch the Ganga Aarti at Haridwar, take yoga retreats, go white-water rafting, and find inner peace.

🗓️ sep – nov ⭐ Spiritual Centre 🕐 2–3 days
🌴 Kerala

Kerala Backwaters

Glide through 900 km of interconnected canals, rivers, and lagoons on a traditional kettuvallam houseboat. Watch village life unfold on the banks of Alleppey.

🗓️ Nov – Feb ⭐ UNESCO Biosphere 🕐 1–2 nights
🛕 Tamil Nadu

Madurai & Meenakshi Temple

One of India's oldest living cities, Madurai centres around the spectacular Meenakshi Amman Temple with its towering, intricately carved gopurams in vivid colours.

🗓️ Oct – Mar ⭐ Ancient Temple City 🕐 2 days
🏰 Karnataka

Hampi — Ruins of Vijayanagara

A UNESCO World Heritage Site scattered with boulder-strewn landscapes and the majestic ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire — India's most atmospheric ancient city.

🗓️ Oct – Feb ⭐ UNESCO World Heritage 🕐 2–3 days
🏖️ Goa

Goa Beaches

India's beach paradise stretches 100km of golden coastline. From lively Baga and Calangute to serene Palolem and Agonda — there's a beach for every mood.

🗓️ Nov – Feb ⭐ Beach & Party Capital 🕐 3–7 days
🏙️ Maharashtra

Mumbai — Maximum City

India's commercial capital never sleeps. From the Gateway of India and Bollywood studios to Dharavi, the Dabbawala system, and the best street food on earth.

🗓️ Nov – Feb ⭐ Financial Capital 🕐 2–4 days
🫖 West Bengal

Darjeeling Tea Country

Ride the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway toy train through tea gardens as Kanchenjunga looms overhead. Sip the world's finest first-flush Darjeeling tea.

🗓️ Mar – May, Sep – Nov ⭐ UNESCO Toy Train 🕐 2–3 days
🏝️ Andaman Islands

Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Crystal-clear turquoise waters, white sand beaches, and some of Asia's best snorkelling and diving. Radhanagar Beach is consistently ranked among the world's best.

🗓️ Oct – May ⭐ Marine Paradise 🕐 5–7 days
